Frequently Asked Questions

What is Johnstone Supply?+
Johnstone Supply is a leading wholesale distributor of HVACR (heating, ventilation, air conditioning, and refrigeration) parts, equipment, and supplies. We serve contractors, technicians, and businesses with a wide range of trusted name brands and products.
What does 'wholesale only' mean, and who can buy from Johnstone Supply?+
We serve licensed HVACR contractors, businesses, and professionals. Wholesale means that we typically do not sell directly to homeowners or the general public!
How do I make an account with Johnstone Supply?+
You can apply online through this link, or visit your local branch to get started!
Do you provide services other than HVAC parts and equipment?+
Yes! Aside from HVAC parts and equipment, we also carry refrigerant and plumbing products! We pride ourselves in being your 'one stop shop', and if we don't have it on hand, we can get it delivered faster than the other guy can.

Aside from that, we also provide live technical support, warranty processing, training and events, and educational courses to help not only understand the product, but to help run your business efficiently.
Can I order online?+
Yes! You can place orders, check pricing and availability, and more once you get your online account set up. You can even download the Johnstone Supply OE Touch app to order through your phone!
